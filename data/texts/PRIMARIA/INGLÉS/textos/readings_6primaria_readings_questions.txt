6º - Reading 2 (6P-02)
The Crazy Restaurant Day

Last Saturday, Emma and her cousin Jack went to a little restaurant. The chef, Mr Crumble, was friendly but a bit forgetful!
First, they ordered pasta and cheese pie. “Is the pie hot?” Jack asked. “Yes! I used the oven, not the microwave!” said Mr Crumble.
Then, Emma wanted some bread, but there wasn’t any! “Don’t worry,” said the chef. “I have flour, I can make more!” He mixed the flour with milk and baked it. It was delicious!
Suddenly, the dishwasher broke and water went everywhere. “Oh no!” said the waiter. But Mr Crumble laughed and said, “Now the floor is clean!”
For dessert, they had chocolate cake with peanuts. It was sweet and crunchy. Jack took a photo and said, “Best lunch ever!”
Before they left, Emma opened the fridge and said, “You have ten cakes!” Mr Crumble smiled, “Shh! They’re for me!”

The Big Race

Leo said, “I’m fast, but Bob is faster than me!”
Carla smiled, “But I trained yesterday! I think I’m going to be the fastest today!”
They all started running. Bob ran across the grass, but he got distracted by a butterfly. Carla was quicker than Leo, but she slipped on a leaf.
Leo didn’t stop. He ran and reached the finish line first!
“I’m the luckiest today!” he said.
Bob arrived last but was the happiest because he found a sausage near the bench.
“I think Bob is the funniest runner,” said Carla, laughing.
